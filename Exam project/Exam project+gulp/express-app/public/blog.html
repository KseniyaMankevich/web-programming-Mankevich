THE MOST CREATIVE CAKES, THAT ARE TOO COOL TO EAT

Posted on 20 Apr,2018

For us food presentation is important.These cakes are perfect examples – we can't taste them,but most of them look as delicious as can be.

Some of us eat with our eyes as well as our mouths, so for us, food presentation is as important, if not more so, than the taste. These cakes are perfect examples – we can't taste them (unfortunately), but most of them look as delicious as can be.Most of these cakes owe their artistic magic to fondant, a controversial cake decoration ingredient that is delicious to some and foul to others. Fondant can be made in various colors and consistencies, so it is the perfect foundation for incredible sculpted cakes like these.Whether its World Baking Day (May 17th) or just a regular Tuesday, there's no reason not to eat some cake. Sure you could satisfy your sweet tooth with pies or cupcakes or cookies, but would those confections really let your flex your creative side? No way! Let this list of crazy cakes inspire you to get your own work of art cooking in the kitchen!Whether you choose chocolate, red velvet, coconut, or Funfetti, you'll need to make sure you dress your cake up. Icing works great, but you're gonna need to stock up on the fondant if you want to play in the big leagues of cake design. You'll also probably need to take a few classes, or maybe just watch a few (or thousands) of YouTube instructional videos.These amazing cakes are way over the top... in a good way! Honestly, the makers of these elaborate cakes must have MFAs. But there's no reason to be intimidated because this list also contains a couple of easier ideas that, according to the directions, you can do at home. We'll believe it when we see it... Regardless of how expert or easy you want to get with your cake baking and decorating skills, make sure you take a look at this list of extravagant cakes before you get started. Vote up the craziest and coolest designs that you think take the cake!
