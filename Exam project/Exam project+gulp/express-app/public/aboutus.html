Our past:

Our bakery was opened in 2003 and has been one of the most famous bakeries for almost 15 years. The bakery was opened at the site of the former postal office.

But its opening was constantly postponed due to permanent construction works nearby.But now it is located in one of the most prestigious areas of the city.A bakery is a family business that is inherited. Therefore, it changes its name and at the moment it is called "Ksu's bakery".

Our now:

Our bakery offers a variety of tastes and cuisines: from Russian and European to home-style American. We produce fresh, top quality goods made from scratch in our modern kitchen. From gourmet truffles, pastries, and cookies, to extravagant wedding cakes, Ksu's bakery offers delicious treats for every occasion and celebration.

Our future:

Soon there will be the opening of another our bakery, which will have a very unusual design, that is a trend in the world of confectionery production.

The ceiling of the room will be decorated with juniper balls, resembling buns or koloboks. By the way, not only the ceiling, but the whole interior of the bakery will have "tasty" honey, caramel, chocolate and golden tones and will favor the appetite.
